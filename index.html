About Curoria

Making academic research accessible, on both sides of the study

Curoria is a nonprofit initiative designed to make academic research more accessible — helping researchers find participants, and helping volunteers contribute to discovery.

Accounts here are handled by Supabase Auth (including Google sign-in), so logins are real and secure once this site is deployed with a live Supabase project.
